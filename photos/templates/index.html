Do you have any photos of Key Club members at our events? If the answer is yes, then this site is for you! We offer points to members that take photos during our events, which can then be used in our monthly newsletters and scrapbook for LTC.

However, most importantly, make sure that the photos or videos that you upload are relevant to the event.
